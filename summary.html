Book Summary

*Howl's Moving Castle* by Diana Wynne Jones is a whimsical fantasy novel that follows Sophie Hatter, a young woman cursed by the Witch of the Waste to live as an old lady. Seeking to break the curse, Sophie stumbles into the moving castle of the mysterious wizard Howl. Alongside Howl, his apprentice Michael, and the fire demon Calcifer, Sophie embarks on an adventure filled with magic, self-discovery, and unexpected friendships.

The story weaves themes of courage, love, and identity against a backdrop of a fantastical world where castles walk and spells abound. Want to know more about my journey with this book? Check out the About Me page!
